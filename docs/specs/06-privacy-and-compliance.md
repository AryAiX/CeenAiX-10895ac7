# 6. Data Privacy, Consent & Compliance Framework

CeenAiX operates under a **consent-first architecture** fully aligned with Dubai Health Authority (DHA) regulations, UAE Federal Data Protection Law, and international standards equivalent to HIPAA.

---

## 6.1 Core Consent Principles

- All AI recording during consultations requires **explicit patient consent** before activation
- Audio recordings are processed and discarded after transcription; only text transcript and structured clinical data are retained, per patient-configurable retention policies
- Patients have **full visibility** into who has accessed their records and can **revoke access at any time**
- Document and photo uploads in guest mode are **session-scoped** and not retained after the session unless the user creates an account
- Family member access requires **explicit consent** from the patient (or legal guardian authority for minors)

## 6.2 Data Residency & Security

- All patient data stored in **UAE-hosted cloud infrastructure**, meeting DHA data residency requirements
- **End-to-end encryption** for all data in transit and at rest
- **Role-based access control (RBAC)** ensures clinical data is accessible only to authorized users with a legitimate care relationship
- Regular **third-party security audits** and DHA compliance certifications

## 6.3 AI Transparency

- All AI recommendations are **labeled as AI-generated** and are advisory — final clinical decisions rest with licensed healthcare providers
- Patients can view a **log of all AI interactions** involving their health data
- **Explainable AI**: AI diagnosis suggestions include a plain-language rationale

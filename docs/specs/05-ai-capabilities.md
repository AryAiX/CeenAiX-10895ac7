# 5. CeenAiX AI Agent — Comprehensive Capability Matrix

The AI agent is the intelligence layer embedded across all platform surfaces. It operates in guest mode (no authentication) and authenticated mode with full access to the user's health record and context.

| Capability | Available To | Requires Account | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conversational Health Consultation | Guest, Patient, Family Member | No | ChatGPT-style natural language conversation about health concerns, symptoms, medications, and wellness |
| Symptom Assessment | Guest, Patient, Family Member | No | Structured symptom interview with preliminary assessment and recommended next steps |
| Doctor & Specialty Matching | Guest, Patient | No (booking requires account) | AI recommends the right specialist and specific doctors based on symptoms, preferences, and location |
| Document & Photo Analysis | Guest, Patient, Family Member | No | Upload photo (ID, insurance card, prescription, lab result) — AI reads, interprets, and assists with form-filling |
| Medication Interaction Check | Patient, Doctor, Pharmacist | Yes | AI checks prescribed or queried medications for interactions, contraindications, and dosage guidance |
| Pre-Visit Assessment | Patient, Family Member | Yes | AI conducts structured pre-visit questionnaire; shares summary with doctor |
| Live Consultation Transcription | Doctor | Yes (Doctor role) | AI listens to consultation and transcribes in real time with patient consent |
| Real-Time Diagnosis Suggestions | Doctor | Yes (Doctor role) | AI analyses live conversation and patient record to surface differential diagnoses and clinical alerts |
| SOAP Note Generation | Doctor | Yes (Doctor role) | AI auto-generates structured clinical notes from consultation transcript |
| Lab Result Interpretation | Patient, Doctor, Nurse | Patient: No for upload; Yes for history | AI interprets lab results, flags abnormals, compares to prior results, explains in plain language |
| Preventive Care Risk Profiling | Patient | Yes | AI analyses history, family history, lifestyle, and environment for personalized risk profile and screening schedule |
| Prescription & Form Auto-Fill | Patient, Family Member | No (upload) / Yes (save) | AI reads uploaded documents and assists in auto-filling platform forms |
| Wellness Coaching | Patient | Yes | AI provides personalized coaching on nutrition, exercise, sleep, and stress management |
| Emergency Profile Summary | Emergency Responders (via Emirates ID) | Patient must be registered | AI surfaces critical health summary for emergency responders via Emirates ID lookup |
| Multilingual Support | All users | No | AI communicates fluently in Arabic, English, Urdu, and other supported languages — auto-detects preference |
